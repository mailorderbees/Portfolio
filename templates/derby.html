About Roller Derby!


My skates. I love these things

Programming isn't all I like to do! Since January of 2018, I have joined a roller derby league! Assassination City Roller Derby!

What is roller derby? Basically it's a full-contact sport played on quad wheel roller skates and it's a ton of fun! Basically each team fields some blockers and a jammer. The jammer tries to lap the other team's players, faster than the other team's jammer, but the blockers try to prevent that. That's where the "full-contact" aspect comes in. I've seen some unaware jammers go flying at a big hit. It's all for fun, though, and I'm loving the community around it, as well as skating myself. It's great exercise, and makes me feel good.


The most efficient path around the track, a rough diamond shape

To be eligible for league play, and to earn your derby name, you have to have a set of evaluated skills such as stops and skating backwards, know the rules and pass a rules test, and then...the dreaded 27/5. That's skating 27 laps around the derby track in 5 minutes. That one's daunting. So far I've made 27 in 5:41 as my best. It's a test of endurance, balance, and will. By the end of it, your lungs and legs are burning! You end up going close to a mile in 27 laps, and you have to maintain a fast enough pace to make it in 5 minutes.

Derby names are always fun; you can pick something that can show off a bit of a playful personality, along with a number you pick out too. The name I want is "Sailor Doom".
